Q1. What is Cyber Law? What are the major areas covered under Cyber Law?

Answer:

Cyber Law is the legal framework governing computers, internet usage, and digital communication. It regulates online activities and protects users from cybercrimes. Major areas include cybercrime, data protection, privacy, intellectual property rights, e-commerce, digital signatures, and cybersecurity.

Q2. What are the major objectives of Cyber Law?

Answer:

Objectives of Cyber Law include preventing cybercrime, protecting privacy and personal data, regulating online activities, promoting secure digital transactions, and ensuring a safe cyberspace for individuals and organizations.
